About Us

HeyFo is an innovative application designed to make it easier for users to find cooking recipes based on the food ingredients they have. The main concept of HeyFo is to utilize scanning technology to identify food ingredients around the user.

Ways of Working

Users simply take a photo or scan the food ingredients available at home or in the shop. After the scanning process is complete, HeyFo will use food ingredient recognition technology to identify these ingredients. Next, based on the ingredients that have been identified, HeyFo will present a list of suitable recipes that can be made with the ingredients that the user has.
